Manage your complete health profile — blood group, allergies, chronic conditions, height, weight, and emergency contacts — all in a secure, easy-to-update personal dashboard.

Register & Set Up Profile

Create your free account in under a minute. Fill in basic health details like age, blood group, and medical history to personalize your experience.

2

Describe Your Symptoms

Chat with HealSmart AI in natural language — say "I have a headache and fever". The bot asks smart follow-up questions and lets you select multiple related symptoms at once.

3

Get AI Prediction

Our trained machine learning model analyses your symptom pattern and predicts the most likely condition with a confidence score, detailed description, and recommended precautions.

Find & Book a Doctor

Browse specialists relevant to your condition. Book a consultation slot directly — the doctor reviews and confirms your appointment through their dashboard.

5

Follow Wellness Plan

Receive your personalized diet plan and yoga routine. Track your health journey and stay informed with condition-specific guidance.
